Parametric Research Articles

These articles are a portion of the research conducted by the Alexandria team. The papers are compiled and written using an LLM, and edited by hand. The purpose of these papers is to showcase the relationship between parametric design work and artificial intelligence. As AI models become more sophisticated, they will be able to assist with more and more complex parametric and computational design workflows. This page is a work in progress.
