Curve Attracted Voronoi

Project Details

This voronoi surface is constructed by populating a geometry with a random collection of points. The points define the shape, size, and location of each of the voronoi cells. From there, a curve is set to determine the scale of the interior aperture of each of the cells. In this case, the closer the voronoi cell is to the given curve, the larger the aperture.
